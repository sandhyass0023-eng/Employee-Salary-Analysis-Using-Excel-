# 📊 Employee Salary Analysis using Excel

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee salary data across different departments using Microsoft Excel. The main objective is to identify salary patterns, workforce trends, and performance insights to support better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze salary distribution across departments
  
- Compare average salaries between departments
  
- Identify workforce trends (employment type, education, etc.)
  
- Evaluate department performance
  
- Build interactive dashboards using Excel
  
- Generate business insights and future predictions

---

## 📁 Dataset Information
- Rows: 1200
  
- Columns: 17
  
- Source: Kaggle
  
- Data includes:
  
  - Employee details
    
  - Department
    
  - Salary
    
  - Job role
    
  - Education level
    
  - Employment type
    
  - City
    
  - Performance score

---

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 💰 Salary Analysis

- Finance department has the highest average salary
  
- HR is the second highest
  
- Marketing follows closely
  
- IT has the lowest average salary

### 👥 Workforce Distribution

- Part-time employees have the highest count
  
- Contract employees are second highest
  
- Full-time employees are the lowest

### 🎓 Education Analysis

- PhD holders are the highest
  
- Master’s degree holders are second
  
- Bachelor’s degree follows closely
  
- High school has the lowest count

### 📈 Performance Analysis

- Finance has the highest performance score
  
- HR is second (very close to Finance)
  
- IT has the lowest performance score

### 🌍 City Insights

- Highest salary: LA
  
- Lowest salary: London
  
- Best performance: New York
  
- Lowest performance: Karachi

### 🧑‍💼 Job Role Insights

- Analyst has the highest salary
  
- Manager is second highest
  
- Developer has the lowest salary

### 🏢 Work Preference

- 52% prefer remote work
  
- 48% prefer non-remote work

---

## 🔍 Final Insights

- Finance is the strongest department (high salary + performance)
  
- IT department needs improvement in salary and performance
  
- Workforce is highly educated (PhD & Master's dominant)
  
- Company relies heavily on flexible workforce (part-time & contract)
  
- Remote work trend is increasing

---

## 🔮 Future Predictions

- IT salaries are likely to increase due to demand
  
- Companies will prefer highly skilled candidates (Master’s & PhD)
  
- Remote work adoption will continue to grow
  
- Organizations may invest more in employee training and development
